    **How to use:**
    Press _Shift + Enter on Windows/Linux_ or _⇧ + Enter on macOS_,
    and you can add a line and place the cursor on it. 
    
    Press _Ctrl + Alt + Enter on Windows/Linux_ or _⌘ + ⌥ + Enter on macOS_
    to add a line above.
