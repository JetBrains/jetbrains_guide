  **How to use:**

  Invoke with _Ctrl + Shift + T on Windows/Linux_, _⌘ + ⇧ + T on macOS_,
  and select **Test for ...** from the popup.

  You can choose to test a _function/method_, _the whole file_, or _the whole package_.
