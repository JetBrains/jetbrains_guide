  **Split Horizontal** and **Split Vertical**.

  **How to use:**

  Invoke the **Split Horizontal** or **Split Vertical** via
  **Find Action**, _Ctrl + Shift + A on Windows/Linux_ or
  _⌘ + ⇧ + A on macOS_, then type the action names you need.
